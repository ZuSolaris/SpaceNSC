Welcome to SEDS @ North

Who are we?

The Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) club is a dynamic community of passionate individuals dedicated to the exploration and advancement of space science and technology. Founded on the principles of curiosity, innovation, and collaboration, our club brings together students from diverse backgrounds and disciplines who share a common fascination with space. Through engaging discussions, hands-on projects, and educational initiatives, we strive to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts and professionals. Whether it's building rockets, organizing stargazing events, or advocating for space exploration, SEDS provides a platform for students to expand their knowledge, develop valuable skills, and contribute to the exciting frontier of space exploration. Join us as we reach for the stars and explore the endless possibilities of the cosmos.
